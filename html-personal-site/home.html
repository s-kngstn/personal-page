This website documents my showcases my work and growth as a web developer. It also serves as a portal to a blog of my personal interests, travels, thoughts, notes and opinions. Tell me more


When I'm not building & breaking things on the web, I enjoy traveling to new places, reading, building PCs, gaming, cooking and learning new things. I have always been fascinated with technology. During this time I was introduced to coding and web developement. Code also came with the added benefit of allowing me to be creative with technology while forcing me to build a tolerance and appreciation for critical thinking and problem solving skills. I think being able to break down any problem and solve it is one of the most important skills we can develop for ourselves. My future goal is to build something that can make a positive impact in others lives.
